3D design

In this chapter i will only learn basic 3D design in unity, if you want to learn advanced 3D design press this link.

Terrain

The first step into build a 3D open world is by creating the ground wich is done by the following steps:

  1. Right click on the Hierarchy window
  2. go to 3D-object > Terrain

In the inspector window you get a few option to use when styling your enviroment

  1. Create Neighbor Terrains, if you want a lage map you could just press an neighboring square and it will expand your map
  2. Paint Terrain, this tool is used to style terrain topology, material, texture
  3. Paint Trees, this tool is used to style and place down trees both individualy and mass placement
  4. Paint Details, this tool works just like Paont Trees and lets you place out singel or multiple objects
  5. Terrain Settings, here you could enable shadow, resolution, materials etc

Paint Terrain - tool

Right beneth the paint Terrain button markt in red is a action bar where you could chose how you want to affect the terrain topology, if you want to higher or lower the ground or maby flaten in out.

Underneth those option you has the paint tool that work in a similar way in all the other terrain styling tools, you get to set your brush stucture, brush size and opacity and then you drag it around the map and press/hold down every time you want to apply the effect

The "Paint Texture" is maby the one that stands out from the rest of the actions most. Additional to the brush you also have to make a terrain layer meaning that you need to add a texture for your brush. They way you are doing that is as following:

  1. Press the "Edit Terrain Layers" button
  2. Press "add layer" - now you should have gotten a popup window loking simmilar to that in the picture below
  3. if you alredy has textures you will se them her and can by dubbletaping one chose that texture

Note/tip/hint: If their not popping up any textures its most likely that you havent any textures downloded but dont wory, its easy to fix, just follow this link to find downloadaple textures and so much more

objects

If you want to build easy objects by yourself Unity has a few built in objects that you could acces through the following steps:

  1. right click on the hierarchy window
  2. navigate to "3D-object
  3. chose what kind of object you want

As with most of the objects in Unity you could also inport them from 3d partys to speed up the design phase

But whatever you make the objects yourself or from a website you will be able to make plenty of modifications in the inspector window and here follow three of what i think is the most usefull ones:

  • Transform - here you could change the position, rotation and scale of your object
  • Mesh Renderer - most of the settings here you could just leave but som intresting is the shadows and lightning
  • Box Collider - uses to determine how two objects will react when they colide

texture

The object has still a boring color and but fixing it is as easy as to navigate to the materials in your project window and draging the material you want onto the object and it will be automaticly changed
